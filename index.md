## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Finance Calc ("the App"), developed by Nexivo Apps ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Finance Calc application on Android devices.

By using this App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. About the App

Finance Calc is a free financial calculator application that helps users calculate EMI, SIP, FD, RD, PPF, and EPF values. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from users. However, our third-party service providers may collect certain information automatically:

### 3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
- IP address (approximate location)
- App usage data
- Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

AdMob uses this data to show relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings:
**Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

For more information, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.2 Google Firebase Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. Firebase may collect:
- App open and session data
- Screen views and navigation patterns
- Device type and operating system
- Country/region (approximate)
- App performance data
- Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

This data is anonymized and used only to improve the App experience.

For more information, visit: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

---

## 4. How We Use Collected Information

The information collected by our third-party providers is used to:
- Display relevant advertisements to support free app development
- Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
- Fix crashes and bugs through Crashlytics reports
- Understand which calculators are used most frequently

Our App is designed for a general audience including users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at appnexivo@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.
